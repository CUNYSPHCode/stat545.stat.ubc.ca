Whether you’re sending an email, or tagging a member of the teaching team, please use this as a guideline for determining who to contact. You can find the contact info of the teaching team under the teaching team section.

Have an issue with the way you were evaluated?

Your TA will have left an Issue on your homework repo. Continue the conversation there.

But I really don’t want anyone else but the teaching team to see my comment!

We still encourage you to muster the courage to continue the discussion in the Issue. Otherwise, send an email directly to the TA that graded your work.

Have an issue with the way you were evaluated, even after discussion with the TA?

Your TA will have left an Issue on your homework repo. Continue the conversation there, this time tagging the instructor.

But I really don’t want anyone else but the teaching team to see my comment!

We still encourage you to muster the courage to continue the discussion in the Issue. Otherwise, send an email directly to the instructor.
